Connect. Celebrate. Lead.

The mission of this organization is to unite Bangladeshi students and community relating TAMU by organizing various cultural events and creating more exposure of history and culture of Bangladesh to the international community by participating in different international activities at TAMU. The purpose is to create a homely atmosphere for the Bangladeshi students and represent socio-cultural heritage of Bangladesh at TAMU.
